About me

Hello im Mumma_bear

I started this adventure with a 24hr live on TikTok to raise awareness for men's mental health. Little did I know how big a response it would receive was huge, so many people reached out for support. MummaBear Mental Health Awareness Team was born, with a great team of moderator's we have managed to get help for people in there area. We have 2 registered psychiatric nurses on our team who volunteer for us, and the rest are great people with big hearts. We have sourced information on mental health services all over the UK, and currently working on information for all over the world
